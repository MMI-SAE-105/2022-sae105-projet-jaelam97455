Have you ever written a letter to yourself? A reply to the person you would become when you go through time. You can go back to your hopes and fears of yesteryear...

But also remember things that have marked you and that you have appreciated! Like a place, a music, a movie or a book.

This is the concept of this site! Here is my point of view on the passage to "adulthood" and the changes it brings. But also the concerns of the relationship with others in our society.

It is up to you to dive in !
